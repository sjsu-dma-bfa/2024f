Dream

Artist Statement

This project, developed using Unreal Engine 5, explores the immersive nature of dreams. The project transports viewers into a virtual representation of my own dream world, divided into distinct layers. In the music level, the audience is encouraged to interact with musical elements, creating a dynamic relationship between sound and space. The nightmare layer presents a stark contrast, with different art styles unfolding within the same environment, offering a more disorienting and surreal experience.

Through this juxtaposition of musical interaction and shifting visual styles,This project aims to convey the emotional complexity of dreamsâ€”moving from serene to unsettling. The project invites the audience to traverse these contrasting layers, providing a deeply personal yet universal exploration of the dream state.

Bio A visionary creator using Unreal Engine 5 to explore the immersive nature of dreams.
