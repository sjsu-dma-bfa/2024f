Disruption

Artist Statement

"Disruption" is a sculptural artwork by Seigfred Causin which addresses the accelerated pace of technology’s adoption in our daily lives. Throughout different works created through 3D modeling software, three different instances of the extremes of living with technology can be seen. The scenes display three different instances that Seigfred sees as some of the most common issues regarding technological modernisation. Causin’s artwork serves as a compelling reflection to our disposable electronic age, showcasing the side effects of those who engross themselves with electronics.

I’ve spent much of my life immersed in technology, and I’ve seen its numerous effects on people as well as have seen the benefits it provides. My affinity towards digital mediums of artworks has caused me to reflect on my relationship towards technology as well as its effects on my behavior. Despite the benefits that modern and mainstream technology offers, there still remain many downsides to the advancement and accessibility of modern technology to vulnerable individuals who fall victim to its integration within their daily lives. Each scene has been familiar to me, whether I’ve felt myself or others close to me succumb to the different depictions in some way.

Seigfred Causin

Bio Seigfred Causin is a digital multimedia artist currently studying at San Jose State University. He was born in San Francisco on May 16th, 2002, and later relocated to Elk Grove, California. Inspired by his fascination with technology, media, and his own creative interests, he became enamored with creating illustrations and photo/video works through computer software. In 2020, he would go on to attend Sacramento City College and study Design and Digital Media for two years until transferring to San Jose State University where he participated in the group exhibition titled “Beyond Words” in 2023 and then again in "Press Play" in 2024. He remains at San Jose State and is currently pursuing a Bachelors in Digital Media Arts.
