Woodland Wanderers

Artist Statement

Set in a dense forest during a storm, this 3D animated short film follows the journey of small creatures navigating through a challenging environment. Their adventure is a reflection of how we, too must confront unexpected changes and obstacles. At the end of the journey, new and exciting things often await the end of the path.
