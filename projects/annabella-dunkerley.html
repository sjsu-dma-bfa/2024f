Artist Statement

Annabella is a digital artist who blends different digital techniques and styles in her work. She enjoys combining illustration with 3D design and creating videos. Her passion for digital art began with her love of video games and animated movies, which inspired her to create 3D art and video projects. Her goal is to design unique characters and environments. Annabella’s projects are technology-driven and utilize a variety of digital tools and techniques.
