Renegade

Artist Statement

Renegade, is a 3D animated short film that explores the journey of a lone warrior finding their own purpose in a hellscape world. Originally called in service of a higher authority, the vagabond traverses the remains of a dying civilization filled with relentless and ruthless foes. During their struggle for survival the vagabond also experiences flashes of their past life. A life filled with companionship and internal peace. The lone warrior is left to cut their own path. This animated short film is inspired by the Dark Souls series, in an attempt to recreate the effective environmental storytelling that allows clear communication of ideas. This project is the culmination of my personal experiences, understanding, and knowledge of the arts. Renegade is a proud milestone in my journey of creating art, challenging perspectives, and expressing ideas.

Daniel Perez Gomez

Bio Daniel Perez Gomez is a Chicano who is proud of his Hispanic and indigenous background. As an artist, I intend to use my talents to spread the silenced stories of our great people. Thus, “here I stand, poor in money, arrogant with pride, bold with machismo, rich in courage and wealthy in spirit and faith.” - Rodolfo Corky Gonzales. Daniel Perez Gomez is also a lazy artist.
