VASQUEZ

Artist Statement

VASQUEZ is a story of vices. The indulgence of self interest, and the cost of it. From my own life, I recall the naivety that once filled my world. As a kid the only thing that mattered was my own interests, and VASQUEZ explores that idea. An exaggerated presentation, but one that answers my own curiosity. Through the means of 3-D animation in Blender, I chose to push how far a person will go to satisfy their desire. Even if that initial spark came from childhood ignorance.
