Niyana Sanders

Bio Niyana Sanders is an emerging digital media artist and social activist from Hayward, California. Sanders works through multimedia in order to bring awareness to societal and political issues. These works include pieces dealing with the Black Lives Matter movement, gun violence, and more. Niyana believes in creating art that makes a difference, while allowing herself to cope with the issues happening throughout the world. She brings forth this essence of change with mediums that include drawing, photography, videography, 3D modeling, games and the Adobe Suite. Sanders believes that the message is far greater than the medium allowing herself to work with whatever medium is available to her at that current moment.
