Alone

Artist Statement

Alone is a 3D Unity game telling a story of an alien coming to earth in pursuit of finding a place to belong. Using transformation, the alien attempts to fit in with other species but fails to coexist. This wildlife game is a representation of someone who has suffered from high levels of anxiety or fear of growing up. I've faced both of these hardships deeply. Negative feelings can mislead people into alienating themselves and believing that something is wrong with them. However, while everyone is unique, we are also the same. This game serves as a reminder to those who isolate themselves by their imperfections: remember, there are other people in the world who have felt the same way.
