Nanotech Warrior

Artist Statement

Nanotech Warrior is a visually dynamic 2D platform video game that explores humanity's complex relationship with artificial intelligence through the lens of classic gaming aesthetics. Set in the floating metropolis of Skyhaven, the game melds retro platforming mechanics with contemporary themes of technological advancement and environmental preservation. Nanotech Warrior was created in Unity with assets created in Adobe Photoshop, Pixsel, and credited found assets.
