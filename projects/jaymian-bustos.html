Death, The Inevitable

Artist Statement

Death, The Inevitable, is a top down rogue-like dungeon crawler where you are sent to the Death’s Domain because they are tired of your heroic deeds and the countless times you’ve cheated them of your life. Now you must escape their deathly grasp by navigating his dungeon, combat with their minions, and defeat Death to escape and return back to life, although they won’t make it so easy…
With this piece, I intend to raise the spirit of competition of the gaming community. People building communities to dissect and solve the game while others keep strategies for themselves to give themselves the best possible odds will create narratives like none other that will ultimately lead to the first person to ever complete the game. It will be a game that recognizes the best of the best despite the task seeming near impossible. Through their failure will eventually come success as long as they keep trying.

Jaymian Bustos
